# Refund Policy v3.2 (effective 2026-01-01)

## Eligibility
- Customers may request a partial credit when an order ships more than 7 calendar days past the committed delivery date.
- Credits are issued as account credit toward future orders, never as cash refund.

## Maximum credit
- Maximum issuable credit for a delayed-shipment case is **50% of the order subtotal**.
- Full refunds are NOT authorized at the support tier. Escalation to the regional manager is required and is out of scope for the support reply.

## Communication rules
- Do not admit fault or liability on behalf of the company in customer correspondence.
- Do not commit to any specific corrective action beyond what this policy allows.
- All credits must be confirmed via the standard "Credit Issued" workflow; the support reply only proposes the credit.

## Tone
- Empathetic, professional, brief. Acknowledge the inconvenience without conceding liability.

## Forbidden actions
- Issuing more than 50% credit.
- Promising a full refund.
- Admitting that the company was at fault.
- Sending the reply automatically — all replies are drafted and held for human review.
